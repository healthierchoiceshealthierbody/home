Around 90% of hospital visits are stress related. Health is the foundation of joy in life because with wealth and no health we can't enjoy life, however without wealth and health we can still enjoy life so why not have both and enjoy life to the fullest. This is why we must prioritize our health and have tools for balancing stress out of our lives. The tool Healthier Choices Healthier Body is providing is a 15-minute practice for letting go of stress.
